Instructions:
  1. Visit the Enouncter Generator at http://skoll.xyz/mythras_eg/
  2. Find your desired enemy and click to visit the template page.
  3. Note the number at the end of the URL.
  4. Go to this url but replace #### with the number noted in step three: http://skoll.xyz/mythras_eg/generate_enemies_json/?id=####
  5. Paste the contents of the page into the textarea above.
  6. Go back to the Sheet tab to enjoy your imported character.
Notes:
  • Once you have generated JSON for one enemy you simply refresh the page to generate a new one.
  • Due to a known bug in Roll20 the importer will not change the character name. This will start working once the bug is fixed.
  • If you are importing a spirit you will need to toggle the Spirit Attributes checkbox manually.
